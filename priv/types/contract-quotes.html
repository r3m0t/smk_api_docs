Represents all of the available visable depth of quotes available for a single contract on both the buy and sell sides within market-quotes being sent to a session.

It can also be sent as a message itself without being wrapped in market-quotes. This represents updates in the market where only the changed quotes will be sent. This is done when an order is accepted or executed where both sides of the market might change like so (using the Smarkets.com depth view as an example UI):
