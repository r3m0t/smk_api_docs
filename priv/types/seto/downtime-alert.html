When the eto/downtime-alert-type of a eto/downtime-alert is set to functionality then this seto/payload and type will also be specified.

The only current functionality downtime is related to order placement and is specified as the seto/downtime-alert-type order-create. Orders will automatically be rejected with reason service-temporarily-unavailable during this downtime until service is resumed via another eto/downtime-alert with type service-resumed.

For the expected datetime of the downtime the eto/downtime-alert's datetime field should be consulted.
